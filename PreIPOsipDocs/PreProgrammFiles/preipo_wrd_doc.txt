## 11. GLOSSARY

**Terms and Definitions:**

- **Pre-IPO**: Shares of a company purchased before its Initial Public Offering
- **SIP**: Systematic Investment Plan - recurring monthly investment
- **BV**: Business Volume - face value of shares allocated
- **Face Value**: Original value of shares at time of allocation
- **Market Value**: Current trading value of shares (after IPO)
- **Progressive Bonus**: Monthly bonus that increases over time
- **Milestone Bonus**: One-time bonus at 12, 24, 36 months
- **Referral Multiplier**: Factor that multiplies all bonuses
- **Lucky Draw**: Monthly prize distribution system
- **Profit Sharing**: Quarterly distribution of platform profits
- **Consistency Bonus**: Bonus for making on-time payments
- **Celebration Bonus**: Birthday/anniversary bonuses
- **Exit Fee**: Fee charged when selling shares (currently 0%)
- **KYC**: Know Your Customer - identity verification
- **Demat**: Dematerialized account for holding shares electronically
- **UTR**: Unique Transaction Reference number for bank transfers
- **DP**: Depository Participant - entity holding demat accounts
